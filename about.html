About Me:

Hello! Thanks for looking at my site, I really appreciate it

I grew up in the Seattle area, went down to Southern California (Long Beach) for college, then moved back up to Seattle in 2018. After college I was lucky enough to get started at Mighty.ai, where I was able to learn from incredible individuals. We were acquired in June 2019 by Uber ATG. And I have been there ever since :)

This is my first attempt at making a live site using HTML and CSS (with a splash of javascript). It all runs on an AWS EC2 using Apache httpd

I have experience using R, Ruby on Rails, and SQL in a production environment and used Stata extensively for statistical analysis in grad school

I like working within a small tight-knit team, on creative problems that require me to learn new things, and to have a significant impact

Some of my favorite things are seeing friends, playing water polo, my family, and globe bars
