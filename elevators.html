Elevators!

I wanted to make a smarter elevator

I felt that elevators waiting a their last floor, waiting to be called was just about the least effiecnt thing they could do

My theroy was that in the mornings, everyone probably wanted to catch an elevator from the first floor on their way to work

And in eveneings, the oppostie would occur. Few people if anybody, was catching an elevator from the first floor, but everyone was going there

So I configued an old financal forcasting model to work with elevators

I used time of day, and floor where the elevator was called as inputs

With the goal of reducing "floors missed"
