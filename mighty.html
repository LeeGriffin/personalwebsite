Where it all began

Alright, that might be a little dramatic

Having said that, it's hard to imagine a better first job out of college. I had found a place where hard work was valued, coworkers who were empathic and helpful, and had a culture that encouraged me to add skills and move within the company

I was able to learn new skills, (project management, R, BPO management), hone skills I already had (SQL, data analysis, working within a team), and learn about the awesome industry that is machine teaching (and self driving cars in particular)

From the christmas parties to the late nights, I remember almost all of it all fondely

Oh, how could I not include the people I worked with in their own section?

I got to work with some amazingly empathic and thoughtful people

They encouraged and helped be develop skills that were helpful to my job at mighty & beyond

They were there with praise during the good times and with support when I needed them

Some of whom I still have the pleasure of working with at Uber ATG

Hopefully, I will get the chance to work with most of them someday in the future

Cheers!
